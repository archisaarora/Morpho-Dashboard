Thesis — Archisa Arora

Morpho is the on-chain credit infrastructure layer institutional finance is choosing to build on. Apollo Global Management ($940B AUM) committed to acquire 9% of MORPHO governance supply over 48 months (Feb 13, 2026) — the first structured governance stake by a top-tier alt asset manager in any DeFi protocol. The fee switch is inactive ($0 protocol revenue in screens), which is the primary forward catalyst: at Aave's 0.15% capture rate on $11.2B deposits, activation implies ~$16.8M annual revenue and immediate re-rating. Entry at current levels offers ~100% upside to my $3.50–$4.00 base case.
